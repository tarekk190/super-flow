🎯 The Framework

The Eisenhower Matrix,
Supercharged by AI

Eisenhower's framework has been trusted by presidents and CEOs for 70 years. SperoFlow adds a real-time AI layer that classifies your tasks with zero manual effort.

Quadrant I
⚡ Do It Now
Urgent & Important. Crises, deadlines, production incidents. Act immediately — but work to shrink this quadrant over time.
Quadrant II
📅 Schedule It
Not Urgent, but Important. Strategy, learning, relationships. This is where all leverage lives. SperoFlow protects this time.
Quadrant III
👥 Delegate It
Urgent but Not Important. Interruptions, requests from others. AI identifies these and suggests who to hand off to.
Quadrant IV
🗑 Eliminate It
Not Urgent, Not Important. Time sinks, busywork. SperoFlow surfaces these ruthlessly so you can cut without guilt.
